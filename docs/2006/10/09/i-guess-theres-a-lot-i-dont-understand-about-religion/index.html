I guess there's a lot I don't understand about religion

Monday, 09 October 2006 2006
I

I feel your pain, Billy. On Saturday I drove down 395 to visit my mom in Louisa (home of alleged deer heads!). On the way there I saw two deeply confusing things.

First, a Calvin window decal on a truck. He wasn't urinating, though â€” he and Susie Derkins were kneeling in prayer before a towering cross. It looked a little like this, except for that sticker's lack of Susie (there are a lot of variations on this decal available on the web).

I guess the message is that there is a power and a love in this world even greater than that of Ford or Chevy. I know that it's hard to open your heart to such a possibility, but the angry piety written on the face of the woman driving the SUV in question told me that I ought to try. Or else.

ALSO: while trying to find that particular decal, I stumbled across this page. I defy you to find a better window into the conservative zeitgeist.

Second, a roadside fruit stand/garden center on Rt. 3 had a 30'-tall silver figurine crucified on an enormous cross. It has "NASA" clumsily written on its thigh. It doesn't seem to exist on Flickr yet, and I missed it on my way home, but this might give you some of the flavor. But think bigger, silverier, and more crucified. Somebody either really likes astronauts or really hates them. That, or they have a serious space-crow problem.
